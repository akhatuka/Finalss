Workshops

Our company offers a variety of workshops to meet the needs our most excited dancers. Our workshops are under the guidance of well-trained instructors who are passionate about dance and focused on helping you enhance your dancing skill.

Beginner Workshops

Our beginner workshops are for those who want to built a strong foundation about dance. This workshop is designed for people who are passionate about learning dance.There are three types of workshop that our company organizes

Ballet Basics Workshop- In this workshop you will learn about the basic techniques used in Ballet. Supportive and encouraging instrcutors. No prior dance experience required.

Conducted on Every Sunday from 5:00PM-6:30PM at Fremont Cultural Center

Enrollment fees- $80

Intermediate Workshops

Our intermediate workshops are for those with some prior experience who want enhance thier existing skills

Contemporary Dance Workshop- In this workshop, we mainly focus on improving flexibility, strength, and control.Prior dance experience recommended.

Conducted on Every Saturday from 4:00PM-5:30PM at Milpitas Cultural Center

Enrollment Fees- $100

Advanced Workshops

Our advanced workshops are for those who want to learn advanced technology, and performing skills.It mainly focuses on reaching your highest potential.

Advanced Hip-hop- In this workshop, we mainly foucs on learning advanced techniques and complex routine involve in hip-hop. We also provide students with opportunities for solo or group performances. Prior dance expereince is required.

Conducted on Every Friday from 6:00PM-7:30PM at San Jose Cultural Center.
